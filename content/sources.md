## Day 1: Understanding Disability and Why Accessibility Matters

### Lecture 1A: Models of Disability

| Source | Type | Relevance |
|--------|------|-----------|
| [Scope UK: Social Model of Disability](https://www.scope.org.uk/about-us/social-model-of-disability/) | Introduction | Clear explanation of the social model from a leading UK disability charity. Good starting point for students new to disability theory. |
| [IAAP CPACC Body of Knowledge, pp. 4–12](https://www.accessibilityassociation.org/sfsites/c/resource/CPACCBoK) | Reference | Comprehensive comparison of medical, social, biopsychosocial, economic, and charity models. The professional certification standard. |

| Kat Holmes: *Mismatch* (ISBN 978-0262539487) | Book | Inclusive design philosophy framing disability as a mismatch between person and environment. Author led Microsoft's inclusive design program. |
